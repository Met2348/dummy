# Priority Reading Order

## Tier 0: Novelty Deciders

Read these in order before changing the proposal:

1. [More Is Not Always Better](../evidence-cards/2605.05716.md): atomic component lattice and model-scale sign flip.
2. [What Should a Skill Remember?](../evidence-cards/2606.09421.md): frozen held-out selector and cross-stack transfer.
3. [Raw Experience to Skill Consumption](../evidence-cards/2605.23899.md): extractor-target matrix, 25% negative transfer, utility rubric.
4. [Agent Psychometrics](../evidence-cards/2604.00594.md): unseen model-scaffold prediction and its additive limitation.
5. [PACE](../evidence-cards/2607.02032.md): strict held-out-model absolute-performance prediction.
6. [SEAGym](../evidence-cards/2606.17546.md): cross-backend sign reversals and ID/OOD mismatch.
7. [Probe-and-Refine](../evidence-cards/2606.20512.md): catastrophic Qwen-to-Nemotron guidance transfer and budget activation thresholds.
8. [MemDelta](../evidence-cards/2606.29914.md): one-factor controls and model-dependent baseline reversal.
