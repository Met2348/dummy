## Author Abstract

Safe anytime-valid inference provides measures of statistical evidence and certainty, including e-processes for testing and confidence sequences for estimation, that remain valid at all stopping times and accommodate continuous monitoring and optional stopping or continuation. These measures rely on test martingales, understood as wealth processes in betting games. The paper summarizes the goals and philosophy of safe anytime-valid inference and reviews advances in testing composite hypotheses and estimating functionals in nonparametric settings.
