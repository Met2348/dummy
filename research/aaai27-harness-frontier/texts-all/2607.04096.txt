Get-Order
System (abbrev.): given a batch of mutually independent
tasks, output a total ordering that best fits the user’s
workflow; output exactly the given ids, inventing no depen-
dencies.
Input: three ready tasks — s5 enable roaming, s6 add 3 GB
data, s7 toggle airplane mode.
Target: {"ordered_task_ids": ["s5", "s6",
"s7"]}

Check-Prerequisites
System (abbrev.): for a prerequisite key that is absent (miss-
ing from scratchpad) or not_met (present but predicate false),
propose at most one unit task that would advance the check
toward Met.
Input: key destination_set, outcome absent; main
task “Navigate to New York after the prerequisite is re-
solved.”
Target:
   {"task": {"tool_name":"set_navigation",
   "parameters":{"destination":"New
   York"}}}
The absent key triggers a single setup task that will populate
it.
