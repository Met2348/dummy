                                                                                                    VI. C ONCLUSION
                                                                            We presented MAVEN-T, a reinforced heterogeneous distil-
                                                                         lation framework for real-time multi-agent trajectory predic-
                                                                         tion. The method separates a high-capacity interaction teacher
                                                                         from a compact deployment student, transfers knowledge at
                                                                         feature, attention, and semantic levels, and refines the student
                                                                         with safety-, comfort-, and progress-aware PPO rewards under
                                                                         a complexity-aware curriculum. Experiments on five public
                                                                         benchmarks and a replay-based closed-loop simulator show
                                                                         that the student can preserve competitive prediction accuracy
