Figure 3: Overview of our skill evolution pipeline. The execution agent Aexec first performs task inference to
generate initial category-level composition skills S0,k and collect execution traces τ , outputs y, and evaluation
feedback si . The optimizer agent Aopt then progressively refines composition skills and creator skills with feedbacks
to obtain Sk⋆ and Ck⋆ . Finally, the merge agent Amerge integrates multiple creator skills into a single C ⋆ .

Metric                              Abb.        Definition
Process Metrics
Execution Error-Free Rate           EE          Successful tool calls / total tool calls.
Clip Merging                        CM          Whether generated clips are merged into a final long video.
Input Processing                    IP          Whether multimodal inputs are understood and preprocessed.
Planning                            PL          Whether the agent produces a plan before generation.
Skill Following                     SF          Proportion of prescribed composition-skill steps correctly followed.
Reference Asset Usage               RAU         Whether user-provided assets are used as required.
Output Metrics
Format Requirements                 FR          Whether hard constraints such as duration, aspect ratio, and resolution are satisfied.
Prompt Requirements                 PR          Whether the generated video satisfies user-specified content and instruction requirements.
Visual Consistency                  VC          Whether characters and scenes remain consistent across clips.
Audio Consistency                   AC          Whether speaker identity and background music style remain consistent across clips.
Audio-Video Consistency             AVC         Whether audio and video are synchronized and all required audio tracks are present.
Plot Logic and Coherence            PLC         Whether the story is understandable and logically connected.
Reference Fidelity                  RF          Whether the final video reflects multimodal references.
