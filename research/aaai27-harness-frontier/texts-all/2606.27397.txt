Action validity and local valuation. To separate
interface-level competence from economic com-
petence, we record whether agents submit valid
structured actions across trading, production, bid-
ding, and item selection. We further inspect rep-
resentative valuation errors by comparing selected
trades and proposals against the game-specific re-
source valuation table, with a focus on Ship-related
valuation mismatches.
