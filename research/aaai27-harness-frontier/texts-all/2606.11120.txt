   Token-based rollouts are sample-efficient on scarce pub-
lic data. Despite training on only seven matches, the
discrete-token, autoregressive trajectory generator adapted
from SMART provides strong best-of-20 forecasting ac-
curacy (Table 1) and supports fully hypothetical rollouts
conditioned on sampled ball flights. This suggests that
language-model-style tokenization is a practical choice
when public sports tracking remains small.
