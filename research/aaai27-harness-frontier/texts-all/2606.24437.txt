D.5    Teacher Prompt for Distillation
The frontier-model teacher (GPT-5.5) receives the
problem, the proposer traces, and the ground-truth
answer; it is constrained to emit labels in the same
JSON schema as the Reviewer. The rationale is
required to justify the score in terms of internal
reasoning quality only, never restating the ground-
truth answer, so that the student learns to judge
without access to ground truth.
