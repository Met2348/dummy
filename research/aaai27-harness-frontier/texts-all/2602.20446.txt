   CTFs can be further tailored to scale in difficult ac-
cording to the target audience. K-12-oriented CTFs are
considered to be the easiest challenges leveraging well-
known security vulnerabilities with only a few exploita-
tion steps (e.g., picoCTF [31]), followed by university-
level CTFs that represent moderate difficulty (e.g., the
CSAW CTF [32]), followed by expert-level CTFs (e.g.,
the DEF CON CTF [33]). As we describe in detail in
Section 4.1, this study focuses on an in-person university-
level CTF that falls on the higher end of the difficulty
spectrum, i.e., it has a larger number of expert-level chal-
lenges than a typical university CTF.
