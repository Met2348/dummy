This appendix specifies the MCTS procedure used in LaPha, including the search space, maintained statistics, the
PUCT selection rule, likelihood-induced priors, value-based initialization, terminal-only backup, and latent-space
pruning.

Search space and statistics. MCTS is performed over step-wise assistant actions. A node corresponds to a
