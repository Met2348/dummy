H    Late-Probe Supersession (continue
     _after_dispatch)
D2 issues a probe after each additional block of CoT, and
Insight 2 shows that probe accuracy rises as the chain-of-
thought unfolds. This raises a design choice: should the con-
troller commit to the first probe that clears the gate, or keep
probing and upgrade its speculation when a later, higher-
confidence probe predicts a different call? We expose this
as a single switch, continue_after_dispatch:
     • OFF (default): the first probe that clears the confi-
        dence gate dispatches the speculative tool and the
        retry loop stops (first commit wins).
     • ON: the controller keeps probing after dispatch; if a
        later probe predicts a different call, it cancels the in-
        flight speculative tool and re-dispatches the new can-
        didate (a supersession).
